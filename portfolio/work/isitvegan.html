Is It Vegan? UI

Client: Is It Vegan?

Brief

Is It Vegan? is a mobile application that features an extensive database of vegan and non-vegan food products. Users are able to scan barcodes or enter them manually and the apps scans the product, lists ingredients, and identifies whether the product is vegan or not. The current UI/UX of this app fails to meet and follow consistent visual guidelines. The intent of this project was to give the UI/UX a new look and feel while focusing on maximizing the efficiency and usability.

Research

For this particular project, I explored the current app's interface and variety of ‘pages’. I compiled a list of effective and ineffective elements and noted what elements to focus on improving. I also explored design principles and determined ways to implement them across the app.

Solution

The majority of the app design is based off design principles from the book Universal Principles of Design. The design principles used are iconic representation, proximity, hierarchy, legibility, alignment and visibility. In combination, all these principles help the app provide the user with well-crafted usability that tailors to their specific needs.
