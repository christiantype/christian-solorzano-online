Morrissey News

Client: Morrissey

Brief

Build online community on social media sites for fans of Morrissey's music. Social media sites should be updated frequently with relevant topics and information. Create Morrissey News' identity to be consistent through various platforms.

Research

There are many similar online communities so I identified what worked for the successful ones and what didn't work for the unsuccessful ones. I investigated what type of social media posts gather the most attention and based my posts off that research and analysis. I also made good use of Facebook's analytics feature and determined what content is the most popular.

Solution

Inspired by the aesthetic of Morrissey's album covers and newsprint media, I created the logo similar to other historical media outlets like the New York Times which use black letter typography. At the moment, the page has over 15,000+ followers and over the years, I've been able to collaborate with other organizations throughout the world to host events, giveaways and contests. There are plans to recruit others to help with managing the pages and expanding onto print media.
