Level Apparel Brand Identity

Client: Level Apparel

Brief

Level Apparel was created and founded in Chicago by Ben Arbin, a vegan musician on a mission to make a difference for the animals. All products are handmade in Chicago. The intent of this project was to create a visual identity that unites humanity with nature.

Research

Part of the research done for this project was to create a moodboard to present to the client and work from there. Level Apparel's merchandise is handmade and local, so the identity needs to reflect that. There's also a strong relationship between producer and consumer so knowing that definitely played an important role in creating the identity.
