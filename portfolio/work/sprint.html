Sprint Advertising Campaign

Client: Leo Burnett

Brief

Leo Burnett recruited students from universities throughout the country in an effort to have them create an effective and compelling ad campaign for their client Sprint. Students were placed in groups and given a creative brief and ordered to create a campaign that advertised their iPhone 5 and Sprint's unlimited data plan. Collaboration with: Rachel Adeniyi, Guadalupe Gomez, Scott Werba

Research

We focused on the three main points from the brief. Our target audience included people between the ages of 18-33. Our main priority was to encourage people to ditch their current phone provider and switch to Sprint. Our method was to list exciting things that our target audience enjoys doing and provide the possibilities that an iPhone with unlimited data can provide.

Solution

Our final solution was to create an ad campaign centered around a competitive social media app that enables users to perform outrageous objectives. The app gives them the opportunity to win a free two-year contract and a iPhone 5. My job as a designer was to create graphics for our campaign and interpret the language into a visual narrative while adhering to the company brand guidelines.
